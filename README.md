##  Project Overview
This project is a Digital Literacy Portfolio created as part of the CSE0001 digital-literacy course. I, being a Student Digital Ambassador at my college, has to help my peers navigate the digital world. The objective is to educate people about digital literacy and to spread awareness on digital communication, online safety and cybersecurity . The main aim of this project is to digitally aware individual, capable of educating others and responsible in using online platforms.

##  Tasks Completed

###  Task 1 – Infographic
Creation of infograph one page visual resource using Canva which helps my batchmates understand what Digital Literacy means and why it matters. The created design shows how digital literacy is not just about using technology, but also about understanding, creating, and staying safe online. The infograph covers what digital literacy is, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices.

###  Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
Creationn and updation of profile on Linkedin, GitHub and Kaggle. Adding my Education section with your current degree, institution, and expected graduation year on Linkedin. Creating a profile README on GitHub named after my username containig my name, branch, year, and one sentence about what I hope to learn. 

###  Task 3 – Platforms
Creating profile on HackerRank and solving 'Solve me First' problem and obtaining problem solving batch. Then create a google form quiz based on digital literacy awareness and understanding containing 4 MCQ type question and one short answer type, Share it and collect response and upload both in platforms folder.

###  Task 4 – Email Etiquette
In order to teach my peers how to communicate safely and respectfully online and to check wheather I can apply email etiquette on myself and communicate rules regarding communicating professionally online to my peers, Two professional email containing a clear subject line, a professional greeting, a well-structured body, and a proper sign-off has to be written. Also a checklist to be created containing 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student. 


###  Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In order to help your peers recognize and avoid online threats, a case study describing: what the crime is, how it typically happens, who is usually targeted, and what the consequences are is done and then 8 tips to stay safe online written including the reporting channel: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and Helpline 1930.
